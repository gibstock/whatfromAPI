WhatFrom?

What is this?

A simple concept born from a simple idea. We're movie trivia junkies at my house and we are constantly on IMDB asking ourselves "What's he from?"

I decided to make an easy API from which I can simply search the name of an actor and return what they've been in without all the Amazon bloat on the IMDB website.

How does it work?

It's as simple and stripped down as it can get. Simply head to RapidAPI to access the endpoint. You will be able to search an actor's name and you will get a JSON object returned with information on the actor.

Use it for your own curiosity or build an app with it and utilize some of the other information returned.
